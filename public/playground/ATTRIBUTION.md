# Playground image credits

## Augmented previews (`vehicle_*.png`, `fish_*.png`)

The PNGs in this directory are **outputs produced with the open-source BNNR library** (augmentations and, for ICD/AICD, saliency + masking) from two **source photographs** (“Urban Traffic” and “Underwater Marine” samples on the site).

**Source photos:** The project repository does not yet record the original author, URL, and license for those two base images. Before using this site or the assets commercially, the team should either:

1. **Document** the exact source (service, URL, license name, and date), or  
2. **Replace** the base images with material under a clear license (e.g. CC0 / public domain on Wikimedia Commons, or photos owned by the BNNR team with permission), then regenerate all derivatives with the same BNNR script so filenames stay in sync with the Playground UI.

Until then, treat the base photographs as **third-party material** and do not assume they are public domain.

## Model used for saliency (ICD / AICD)

Saliency maps shown in the Playground are described on the site as produced with **OptiCAM** on a **pretrained ResNet-18** from **torchvision**, which is distributed under its own license (see the [PyTorch / torchvision](https://github.com/pytorch/vision) project). That covers the **model weights**, not the underlying photos.

## BNNR-generated pixels

Augmentation effects and composite previews are **generated by BNNR** code; the **MIT license** of the BNNR library applies to that code, not to an implied license on unidentified stock photography used as input.
